Career Journey

Shreyas Iyer, known for his elegant stroke play, made his international debut in 2017. Since then, he has become a vital part of India’s middle order with his composed yet aggressive batting.

Leadership

Shreyas has captained the Delhi Capitals in the IPL, leading them to their first-ever final in 2020 with calm and intelligent leadership.

Career Highlights

  • Scored a maiden ODI century against New Zealand in 2020.
  • Known for his consistency in limited-overs formats.
  • One of the best players of spin in modern cricket.

Fun Facts

Shreyas Iyer is known as the ‘Mini Captain Cool’ for his calm temperament.

He scored a double century in the Ranji Trophy final early in his career.
